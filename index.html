Courses designated as Honors receive an additional 0.3 weight. AP (Advanced Placement) courses receive an additional 0.5 weight. College Prep and "Advanced" courses receive no extra weight. Physical Education and Health are not included in GPA calculations.

Step-by-Step GPA Calculation

  1. Convert Letter Grade to GPA: Use the table above.
  2. Add Course Weight:
    • AP = +0.50
    • Honors = +0.30
    • CP/CP = 0
  3. Multiply by Credits:
    Grade Points = (Base GPA + Weight) × Credits
  4. Add Up All Grade Points and Credits for the Year.
  5. Calculate GPA:
    Year GPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Total Credits
  6. Cumulative GPA:
    Add all year totals and divide:
    Cumulative GPA = Σ Grade Points ÷ Σ Credits

How Final Grades Are Determined

At Glen Rock High School, final course grades are determined by calculating a weighted average of Grade Point Average (GPA) equivalents assigned to letter grades earned during each marking period and, if applicable, the final exam.

Step 1: Convert Percentages to Letter Grades

Each marking period grade and final exam (if applicable) is first converted to a letter grade based on the official percentage scale. For example, a percentage between 93 and 96 results in a grade of A. A percentage of 97 or above results in an A+.

Step 2: Convert Letter Grades to GPA Values

The letter grade for each marking period is then assigned a GPA value according to the district’s conversion scale. For example, an A is equivalent to a GPA of 4.0, a B+ to 3.3, and so on.

Step 3: Apply Course-Specific Weights

Full-Year CP and Honors Courses

  • Marking Periods 1–4: 22.5% each
  • Final Exam: 10%

Final GPA = MP1 × 22.5% + MP2 × 22.5% + MP3 × 22.5% + MP4 × 22.5% + Final Exam × 10%

Full-Year AP Courses

  • Marking Periods 1–4: 25% each
  • No final exam

Final GPA = MP1 × 25% + MP2 × 25% + MP3 × 25% + MP4 × 25%

Semester CP/Honors Courses

  • Each Semester Half: 45%
  • Final Exam: 10%

Final GPA = Half 1 × 45% + Half 2 × 45% + Final Exam × 10%

Semester AP Courses

  • Each Semester Half: 50%
  • No final exam

Final GPA = Half 1 × 50% + Half 2 × 50%

Step 4: Assign Final Letter Grade

Once the weighted GPA is calculated, it is matched to a final letter grade using the official GPA conversion table below:
